About Us

We create inspiring spaces

_
McCarthys carry out a wide range of internal and external refurbishment projects, within 'X' sectors. Our experience, expertise and cost effectiveness is your guarantee of a successful project. Our customers range from small local businesses to major construction companies who have worked with us for 20 years. We have refurbished and fitted out new factories, warehouses and offices throughout the UK.

Our client promises

_
Our reputation has been built on consistently delivering on our client promises, which form the basis of all our client satisfaction guarantees. We deliver on our promises and exceed expectations time after time. We monitor and report on our promises to you throughout our project as a measure of overall satisfaction.

_
Hit targets

Milestones agreed at the beginning of the project and managed through regular core team meetings throughout the project to completion.

_
Efficient budget management

The agreed budget is managed closely throughout the project via the core team. You can even access ‘real time’ cost tracking at any point.

_
Eliminate hassle

Works are reviewed on site weekly to minimise snags, all documents and draft test certificates are requested and packaged to produce the OMM prior to project completion.

Our Process

_
Delivering workspaces your people will love Whatever stage you are at on your workspace journey, we are here to provide the support and guidance you need. It is what we believe makes us unique, and why over 86% of our clients come back to us again and again.

_

meet

_
Insight into your workspace We want to know everything about what makes your business and people tick. Initial stakeholder workshops, followed by space utilisation surveys and staff interviews enable us to understand you, your goals, and how to help you achieve them through your workspace.

_
Our partnership begins The first meeting is most frequently used as a fact-finding mission. We want to understand your business and its ambitions, any project drivers, and give you the confidence that McCarthys are the right partner for your project. Typical things we’ll cover are: • Aims & Culture • Legal Duties • Lease-end • Timescales • Future-proofing..
