PLANNING APPLICATION LIAISON

_
In instances where Building Regulation Approval is required, all drawings, engineering calculations and specifications produced by ourselves, are submitted with the relevant application forms to the Local Authority on your behalf.

We respond to any queries that may be raised until the necessary approval is obtained. This is all done on your behalf so no need to worry.

With many years of experience dealing with Building Regulation approval we are very aware of the latest legislation in place and can advise before commencement of any works of the different aspects that will be required, such as Fire Escape Routes, Adequate Smoke Detection, Emergency Lighting, Fire Protection and Ventilation.

SPACE PLANNING

_
Space is the first stage of the process – and the most important to get right. Our designers are experts in maximising the potential of space, no matter how big or small.

_
We know that careful planning is essential right from the start, even on the smallest projects, so we make sure everybody is completely clear on the objectives and the results that are required.

Space planning objectives are broadly to reduce costs, increase flexibility, encourage interaction, support cultural change, stimulate creativity, attract and retain staff, reduce environmental impact, enhance Productivity We really do look at everything when we assess your office needs, whether it’s feasibility studies or building appraisals to help you decide how much space you need actually need.

We make the planning and design process as simple as possible and from our first visit, complete a full measurement survey of the area involved and discuss the specific requirements of your o ce space including crucial choices such as location, use, layout, appearance, filing and standardisation.
